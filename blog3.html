Promotional Packages from Top Affiliate Publishers – Now on Easy Click Network


Businesses constantly seek innovative strategies to enhance visibility, drive sales, and achieve consistent, predictable outcomes. Easy Click Network’s Fixed Promo Placements offer an exclusive and effective solution by providing tailored promotional packages with top-performing affiliate publishers.


What Are Fixed Promo Placements?

Fixed Promo Placements are a premium feature from Easy Click Network that guarantees advertisers prime ad space on publisher platforms for a set timeframe. Unlike traditional performance-based marketing, which can yield variable results, Fixed Promo Placements ensure predictable outcomes and consistent visibility.

With Fixed Promo Placements, your offers are prominently positioned on high-performing publisher platforms, ensuring you connect with your target audience exactly when it matters most.


Types of Publisher Platforms Available

Easy Click Network collaborates with a diverse network of publishers, offering options to suit every campaign’s needs:

  • Coupon and Deal Sites: Ideal for promoting discounts, exclusive deals, and special offers to attract bargain-seekers.
  • Cashback Platforms: Engage shoppers motivated by cashback incentives to drive conversions.
  • Content Websites: Partner with blogs, magazines, and niche platforms like tech, fashion, travel, or fitness for targeted outreach.
  • Price Comparison Sites: Gain visibility among consumers actively researching and comparing product prices
  • Mobile Apps: Advertise on high-engagement apps in shopping, gaming, or lifestyle categories.
  • Social Media Influencers and Creators: Leverage influencer trust to tap into niche audiences.
  • Email Marketing Platforms: Feature your offers in curated newsletters sent to relevant audiences.

By choosing the right publisher platform, you can align your campaigns with your audience’s preferences and shopping habits.


Types of Promo Placements Available

Easy Click Network’s Fixed Promo Placements provide customizable options to meet specific marketing objectives. Popular placement types include:

  • Banner Ads: Showcase visually appealing banners in high-visibility sections of publisher platforms.
  • Featured Listings: Secure priority spots in deal and offer lists to ensure maximum exposure.
  • Dedicated Newsletters: Get direct access to potential customers with targeted email campaigns.
  • Homepage Features: Ensure your offer is front and center with prominent homepage placement.
  • Push Notifications: Send timely alerts directly to mobile users to drive immediate engagement.
  • Sponsored Blog Posts: Publish detailed articles about your products or services on influential platforms.
  • Social Media Shoutouts: Gain exposure through posts and mentions on a publisher’s social media channels.

These flexible options enable advertisers to craft campaigns that meet their unique objectives, whether that’s increasing awareness, driving traffic, or boosting sales.


Why Choose Fixed Promo Placements with Easy Click Network?

  • Guaranteed Visibility
